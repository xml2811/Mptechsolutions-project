# MPTechSolutions

## Digital Services · Business Software · Automation · Hosting · Local Business Technology

**MPTechSolutions** is a digital services and software project focused on helping small businesses, local companies and professionals improve their digital presence, operations and internal management.

Website:

**https://mptechsolutions.es**

---

## Vision

The goal of MPTechSolutions is to become a professional digital hub where businesses can access technology solutions without needing to understand all the technical complexity behind them.

MPTechSolutions is designed to offer:

- Websites
- Online Stores
- Booking Systems
- Business Management Tools
- Automation Workflows
- Server And Hosting Management
- Custom Software
- Future SaaS Products

The objective is not only to create websites, but to build complete digital systems that help businesses operate better.

---

## What MPTechSolutions Is Being Built To Offer

| Service | Description |
|---|---|
| Business Websites | Modern, responsive and professional websites for local businesses and independent professionals |
| Online Stores | Virtual stores, product catalogues, payment flows and e-commerce structures |
| Booking Platforms | Reservation systems for barbershops, beauty centers, clinics, trainers and appointment-based businesses |
| Business Dashboards | Internal panels for managing services, staff, appointments, customers and operations |
| Automation Systems | Automated notifications, email flows, WhatsApp reminders, reports and operational tasks |
| Server Management | Future VPS rental, hosting management, maintenance and deployment services |
| Custom Software | Software solutions adapted to specific business processes |
| Digital Consulting | Technical guidance for businesses that need a clear digital direction |

## Main Product Direction

One of the main internal products is a **Barbershop And Local Business Booking SaaS**.

This product is being designed to provide businesses with:

| Module | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Public Booking Page | Customers can book appointments online |
| Owner Dashboard | Business owners can manage their business |
| Staff Dashboard | Barbers or employees can manage appointments |
| Services Management | Create, edit and organize offered services |
| Appointment Management | View, confirm and cancel appointments |
| Public Business Page | Display business information, services and booking options |
| Notifications | Future email and WhatsApp reminders |
| Billing | Future subscription and billing system |
| Automations | Future n8n workflows and reporting |

## Project Principles

MPTechSolutions is being built with these principles:

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Practical First | Every feature should solve a real problem |
| Simple Before Complex | Avoid unnecessary complexity |
| Business Value | Technology must help the business, not just look good |
| Maintainable Architecture | The system must be understandable and scalable |
| Security By Default | Sessions, roles, validation and privacy matter |
| Mobile Friendly | Business owners and customers must be able to use it from mobile |
| Progressive Development | Build in phases and improve continuously |
| Professional Presentation | The product must look trustworthy and serious |
